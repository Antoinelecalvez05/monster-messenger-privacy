Terms & Conditions — Sentinelle Reddit

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Publisher and scope

Sentinelle Reddit is a private research and technical-evaluation prototype associated with Monster Messenger and operated by the nonprofit association Centres Relier. These terms govern access to and use of the prototype and its public information pages.

Research prototype

The prototype analyzes public Reddit content to evaluate methods for detecting and categorizing potential signals related to cyberbullying and online distress. It is not an emergency service, medical service, diagnostic tool, moderation service or substitute for qualified professional advice.

No automated contact

The current prototype does not automatically publish posts, reply to users or send direct messages. Its outputs are experimental and must not be treated as definitive conclusions about any individual.

Acceptable use

You must not use the prototype or its outputs to harass, identify, profile, discriminate against, threaten or otherwise harm any person; circumvent Reddit safeguards; scrape Reddit; or violate applicable law, Reddit’s terms or third-party rights.
