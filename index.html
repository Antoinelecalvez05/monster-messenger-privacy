Privacy Policy – Monster Messenger Bot

Last updated: July 2026

Monster Messenger Bot is an online safety and cyberbullying support chatbot connected to a professional Instagram account. The service helps people who contact the connected Instagram account receive guidance and support about online harassment, harmful messages, cyberbullying, and digital wellbeing.

1. Who operates this service

Monster Messenger Bot is operated by Centres Relier.

Contact: antoine@centresrelier.org

2. Data we collect

When a person sends a direct message to the connected Instagram professional account, the application may process the following data:

  • the content of the Instagram direct message;
  • the Instagram sender identifier;
  • the conversation or session identifier;
  • the detected language of the message;
  • timestamps and technical logs needed to operate and secure the service;
  • webhook metadata received from Meta or Instagram.

We do not collect data for advertising purposes.

3. How we use the data

We use the data only to:

  • receive and understand messages sent to the connected Instagram account;
  • create and maintain a conversation session;
  • generate an appropriate chatbot response;
  • provide support related to online safety, cyberbullying, harmful online situations, and digital wellbeing;
  • debug, secure, and improve the reliability of the service.

4. AI processing

Monster Messenger Bot may use AI services to generate support responses. Message content may be processed by technical providers used to operate the chatbot and generate responses. These providers are used only to deliver the service.

We do not use Instagram message content for advertising. We do not sell user data.

5. Data sharing

We may share data only with technical service providers that are necessary to operate the service, such as hosting, database, infrastructure, AI response generation, and security providers.

We do not sell personal data. We do not share message content with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data retention

We keep data only for as long as necessary to operate, secure, test, and improve the service, or as required by law.

For development and testing, local test data may be reset or deleted as part of normal technical operations.

7. User rights and data deletion

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their data by contacting:

antoine@centresrelier.org

To help us identify the relevant data, please include the Instagram account used to contact the chatbot and the approximate date of the conversation.

We will review deletion requests and delete the relevant data unless we are required to keep it for legal, security, or abuse-prevention reasons.

8. Children and sensitive situations

The service is designed to provide support and guidance about online safety and cyberbullying. It is not an emergency service. If someone is in immediate danger, they should contact local emergency services or a trusted adult, parent, teacher, or professional.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
